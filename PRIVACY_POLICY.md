# Privacy Policy for App Usage Tracker

**Last updated: December 20, 2025**

## Overview
App Usage Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles your information.

## Information Collection
App Usage Tracker accesses your device's app usage statistics solely to display them to you within the app. 

### We do NOT:
- Collect any personal information
- Upload data to any server
- Share your information with third parties
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, messages, or files
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements

## Usage Access Permission
The app requires the "Usage Access" permission to read app usage statistics from your device's system. 

### This data:
- Stays entirely on your device
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Is stored locally in encrypted format
- Is used only to show you your app usage patterns
- Can be deleted by clearing app data or uninstalling

## Data Storage
All app usage data is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database. You have complete control over this data:
- View your data anytime within the app
- Clear specific entries if desired
- Delete all data by clearing app storage
- Remove all data by uninstalling the app

## Permissions Used

### Required Permissions:
1. **PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS (Usage Access)**
   - Purpose: To read app usage statistics
   - Data accessed: App names, usage duration, last time used
   - Data storage: Local device only
   - Data sharing: Never shared

2. **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**
   - Purpose: To restart widget updates after device reboot
   - Data accessed: None
   - Data storage: None
   - Data sharing: None

## Third-Party Services
App Usage Tracker does not integrate with, use, or share data with any third-party services including:
- Analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Cloud storage services
- Any external servers

## Children's Privacy
Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require any personal information to function.

## Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No network transmission of personal data
- App follows Android's security best practices
- Regular security updates

## Your Rights
You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: View all your usage data within the app
- **Delete**: Clear all data through app settings or device settings
- **Control**: Revoke Usage Access permission at any time
- **Transparency**: This policy explains exactly how we handle data
